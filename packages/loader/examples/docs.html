Installation

One script tag.
A voice adviser, embedded.

Izimvo adds a spoken shopping adviser to any site. It searches a live product catalogue, narrows by voice, gives its opinion, paints product cards, and hands off to checkout — all from a single tag, with no SDK to wire up.

01 Drop in the script

Paste this near the end of your <body>. That's the whole integration — the loader renders its own widget and boots the voice session on first tap.

index.html 
<script
  src="https://izimvo-backend-production.up.railway.app/v1/loader.js"
  data-site-key="pk_live_demo"
  data-category="trail-running"
></script>

In production you'd point src at your CDN build of the loader; the thin entry stays stable, so the tag never changes when the core updates.

02 Configure the tag

The loader reads these data-* attributes off its own script element:

AttributeRequiredWhat it does
data-site-keyRequiredYour public key (pk_live_…). Identifies the site and drives config + billing. Safe to ship publicly — it's not a secret.
data-categoryOptionalPer-page scope, e.g. trail-running. Sanitised and mapped to a known catalogue scope server-side, so the adviser stays on-topic.
data-user-idOptionalAn opaque id for the signed-in shopper. Enables the adviser to remember them across sessions.
data-localeOptionalBCP-47 locale, e.g. en-ZA. Falls back to your site default.

03 Allowlist your domain

Every session is minted server-side and bound to your domain: the request's origin must exactly match a hostname registered to your site-key, or it's refused. This is what stops anyone copying your key and running up your bill from their own site.

Getting set up: you'll be issued a site-key and we register your domain(s) (e.g. shop.example.com). Until your domain is on the allowlist, the widget loads but session requests return 403. Your catalogue categories (the values you put in data-category) are configured at the same time.

04 What your visitors get

  • Tap to talk. A small orb sits in the corner. Audio and the mic only start on a tap — never before — so it's iOS-safe and unintrusive.
  • It advises, it doesn't list. The adviser narrows by voice and reads at most three options aloud, leading with the one it'd pick and why. Cards on screen carry the detail.
  • Grounded in real stock. Every spoken product fact comes from live catalogue data — no invented SKUs or prices.
  • Hands off to checkout. When the shopper's decided, it opens the merchant's checkout link (attribution-tagged) in a new tab.

05 What it never does

  • No secrets in the browser. The bundle only ever holds your public key and a short-lived session token. API keys stay on our servers.
  • No style bleed. All widget UI lives in a Shadow DOM — your CSS can't leak in, and the widget can't leak out onto your page.
  • No storage. Nothing is written to the browser beyond in-memory session state.
